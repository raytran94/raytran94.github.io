03 · Entering & Leaving

Door Protocol

Every Time
⚠️
Escape Artists
Vela in particular will bolt through an open door without hesitation. Never let your guard down — always confirm both cats are inside before leaving.
  • When entering, block the doorway and ensure Vela and Marcelo don't slip into the lobby
  • If a cat escapes into the lobby, bring them back immediately — other units may have dogs
  • Watch for both cats near the door before leaving — if they're hovering, they're planning an escape
  • Before leaving: confirm both Vela and Marcelo are inside — they can sneak out unnoticed
🚫
Balcony is Off Limits
Please keep the balcony door shut. Vela has escaped to the neighbour's balcony before and will do it again. Do not open the balcony door under any circumstance.

08 · Kitchen & Coffee

Cooking & Coffee

🍲
The Staub Cast Iron Pots (red)
No metal utensils — it'll damage the enamel. Don't drop them, they're heavy. Always start on low heat and pre-heat before turning up. High heat from cold will destroy the coating.
✅
When in doubt, use the red Francis Francis machine
It's almost like a Nespresso — simple and consistent. ESE pods (Kimbo) are provided.
  1. 1
    Turn on the power button on the machine
  2. 2
    Fill the reservoir on the right side with water
  3. 3
    Wait for a beep and the green light to come on
  4. 4
    Insert a Kimbo ESE pod
  5. 5
    Press the coffee button — wait until it beeps multiple times to finish. Enjoy!
⚙️
Cafelat Robot — Two baskets available
Pressurized basket — uses pre-ground coffee, skips steps 2–4 below. Regular basket — uses fresh ground beans (steps below are for this method).
  1. 1
    Boil water
  2. 2
    Turn on scale, grab a tea cup from the drawer and tare the scale
  3. 3
    Pour out 18g of coffee beans
  4. 4
    Grind using the Kingrinder K6 — it's set to standard, don't adjust the grind setting
  5. 5
    Empty grounds into basket, tamp firmly, add the metal screen on top
  6. 6
    Insert basket into portafilter
  7. 7
    When water boils, pour it into the portafilter
  8. 8
    Raise the robot arms, insert portafilter and twist to lock (left or right)
  9. 9
    Place scale + cup under, tare and start timer
  10. 10
    At 0:04 — draw arms down to 2 BAR
  11. 11
    At 0:10 — increase to 8 BAR and yield 36g of espresso
  12. 12
    Remove cup, add the bean-measuring cup, do another draw to flush remaining water
  13. 13
    When extraction fizzes out, lift arms, untwist portafilter. Clean and enjoy with milk!

11 · Emergencies

Emergency Protocols

Important
🏥
Vet Emergency — PetNation Animal Hospital (24hr)
445 King Street East, Toronto, ON M5A 1L5 · Open 24 hours

Contact us first with the cat's condition. If it looks urgent — go immediately. We will cover all costs, just keep receipts.
Carriers
Both cats have a carrier — in the front closet on the top rack (or left out). Marcelo → black duffel bag. Vela → backpack.
Pee pads
Blue pee pads under the kitchen sink — use them inside carriers if you're worried about accidents
🐱
If a cat escapes
Bring them back immediately. If inaccessible (e.g. neighbour's balcony or unit), knock on their door or call building security/concierge. If Vela escaped to the balcony, shake treats or food to lure her back — do not open the balcony door from outside.
💧
If a cat pees or poops on something
This usually means the litter isn't being maintained — scoop more often. Solutions by item:
Bed
Remove soiled items, wash on hot with OxiClean (above dryer)
Mattress protector
Try washer/dryer — or replace from IKEA (we'll reimburse)
Blanket
Take to a laundromat (it's bulky). Alternatively, leave outside on the balcony and use any living room blankets in the meantime
Towels
Wash with OxiClean. Tip: hang towels behind the washroom door (not on the towel rack) to prevent accidents
Floor
Wipe and spray with the cleaner left out, wipe with paper towel
Green carpet
Place it outside on the balcony
Red carpet
Buy enzymatic cleaner and do your best — we'll deal with it when we're home
